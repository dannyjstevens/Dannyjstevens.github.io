WHAT ORIGINALLY MADE YOU WANT TO BECOME A DESIGNER?

It started when I was 8 years old when my brtoher ripped up all my artwork. It was heartbreaking but that's when I realized how passionate I was for creating and being an artist. At the age of 18 I became an entrepreneur. I franchised four Dippin Dots Ice Cream locations within four years. What I loved about that was the creative process of making ads to get customers through the door. After crushing the ice cream world, I followed my passion to San Diego State University where I study visual and web design.

WHAT KIND OF DESIGN DO YOU WANT TO DO AFTER YOU GRADUATE?

I want to continue doing UX design for Hookit in Solana Beach. The company was established in 2011 and they are continuing to grow.

WHAT IS UX DESIGN AND WHAT MADE YOU CHOOSE UX?

UX stands for user experience. I thought I wanted to be a graphic designer until the summer of 2015. My interest in startups led me to the Startup Institute in Boston, MA. where we focused on UX design and research. When I returned to San Diego, CA. I obtained a UX design postion at HookIt.

Who inspires you and your designs?

It's not a single person that inspires me but it's everyone around me. I surround myself with people with positive energy.

What kind of work do you do as UX designer?

My main focus right now is user flow on our desktop, tablet and mobile sites. I have also been conducting in person user testing to observe the user's behavior. My goal is to create the best user experience for our users.

>

WHAT TYPE OF PROJECT DO YOU LIKE WORKING ON THE MOST?

I really love working with the innovative team to come up with new product ideas and how we can implement them within the company. My favorite phase of the project is the design sprints that we perform. These projects can run anywhere from two-five days and the process is very fun but exhausting. Every time a design sprint is completed I look at what we finished and I think to myself, would you look at that, just look at it.
