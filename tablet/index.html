Hi My name is Daniel

 

Business development and design is at my core. My first entrepreneurial venture began after high school, as I franchised four Dippin Dots Ice Cream locations within four years. After such an amazing hands-on learning experience that reawakened my love of design in all forms, I went to San Diego State University to pursue a degree in multimedia. All the while, I have kept my hands in the business world through managing restaurants and participating in an eight-week web and UX design program at the Startup Institute in Boston in the summer of 2015. I currently reside in San Diego and continually look to make a difference for users utilizing my visual design skills.
